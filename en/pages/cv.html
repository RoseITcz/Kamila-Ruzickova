Provesní životopis PhDr. Kamila Růžičková, Ph.D.

Kamila Růžičková specializes on the field of education and rehabelitation of persons with visual impairment. Her research and publication activities focuse on education and rehabelitation of people with visual impairment. She works as a special educationer and consultant at Special Education centre for children with visual impairment in Hradec Králové. Dr. Ruzickova teaches the subjects typhlopedy, rehabelitation and special education at the Univerzity of Hradec Králové - Department of special education and speech therapy. She also holds the position of tutor for students with special needs and English speaking students. She collaborates on designing supporting services for spudents with special needs at the Augustin centre of the University of Hradec Králové.
